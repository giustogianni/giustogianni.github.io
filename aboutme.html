About me

Short summary.

I started my Bachelor in Life Sciences Engineering at EPFL in 2015 and graduated in 2018. The computing courses followed during this period raised my interests towards Data Science and motivated me to deepen my knowledge in this area of study. Therefore, since then, I mainly follow courses related to data and computer sciences (see here) while keeping an interest in neurosciences. Throughout my Master, I had the opportunity to work on a wide variety of projects which required different skills. I have in particular knowledges in Python: basic libraries such as Numpy, pandas and Scikit-Learn but also with deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch and Keras. The Hadoop ecosystem is also familiar to me as I worked on projects involving HDFS, Spark, MLLib, Hive and Kafka. In addition, the data visualization course gave me the occasion to learn common web technologies for developers: HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
